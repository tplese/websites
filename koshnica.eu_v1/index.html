ABOUT KOSHNICA

We are an employment placement agency based in Croatia and Ireland. We aim to connect employers in Ireland with job seekers from Croatia interested in starting a new life in Ireland. Our focus is on activities of online employment placement but we also offer other connected services.


Main reasons we think this is a good connect:
Ireland

Ireland needs workers. Especially in Healthcare, Technology, Science and Construction sectors.

Ireland

Just for Construction sector, estimates go as high as 112,000 new workers needed.

Croatia

Croatia has unemployment rate of 11%. Education levels are at 61.6% for medium (levels 3-4) and 25.5% for higher education (levels 5-8).

Croatia

English is taught from 1st grade in Croatia. 97.1% of students learned it at their lower secondary level in 2012.
