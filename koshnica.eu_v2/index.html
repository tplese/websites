WHY KOSHNICA?

Licenced Employment placement agency.

We aim to serve as communication line between those who offer jobs and those who seek jobs.

Offices in Croatia and Ireland.

Our aim is to connect Irish employers with Croatian job seekers.

Access to 250,000 job seekers.

Croatia has high unemployment rate. Because 97.1% of secondary level students learn English, Ireland is an attractive option for many job seekers.

End-to-end recruitment.

You provide us with details of what your recruitment needs are and we find complete solution tailored to your needs.

Highly proffessional approach.

Our core belief is that if you're going to do something you need to do it right. At Koshinca we do our best to live by that rule.
